What is the MudeMeter®?

Close
The MudeMeter Score – based on the published opinions of hundreds of film and television critics – is a trusted measurement of movie and TV programming quality for millions of moviegoers. It represents the percentage of professional critic reviews that are positive for a given film or television show. We share same the Medal System for both critics and users. However the scale & the icon are different, M for crtics and m for users.
